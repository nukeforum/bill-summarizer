Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-04

Informed Citizen is an Android app that displays U.S. Congressional bill information. This policy describes what the app does and does not do with your data. Plain version: the app does not collect personal information, has no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party tracking.

What the app stores on your device

The only setting the app persists is your selected theme (system, light, or dark). It is stored locally using Android Jetpack DataStore and never leaves your device, except as part of the standard Android Auto Backup that your device manages with your Google account.

What the app sends over the network

  • Bill manifest. The app downloads a public JSON file from nukeforum.github.io/bill-summarizer/data/bills.json, hosted on GitHub Pages. This is a plain HTTPS request for a public file; no personal information is sent. GitHub may log standard request metadata (IP address, user agent) per their own privacy statement.
  • Congress.gov links. When you open a bill on Congress.gov from inside the app, the link is handed off to your device's browser (via Chrome Custom Tabs). From that point on, the request is made by your browser, not the app.

The "Share to LLM" feature

The app can build a prompt containing the bill's public metadata and text excerpt, and send it to ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, or any app you choose via Android's share menu. This action only happens when you tap a share button. The content of the prompt comes from public Congressional records plus the prompt template; it does not include any information about you. Once the prompt is handed to another app or website, that app or website handles the data under its own privacy policy, not this one.

What the app does not do

  • No user accounts, sign-in, or authentication.
  • No analytics or telemetry SDKs.
  • No crash reporting that leaves the device.
  • No advertising or ad-network integration.
  • No collection of location, contacts, photos, microphone, camera, or any other sensitive permission. The app's only declared permission is INTERNET.
  • No selling or sharing of personal information; we do not have any to sell or share.

Children's privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children.

Source code

Informed Citizen is open source. You can verify the claims above by reading the source at github.com/nukeforum/bill-summarizer.

Changes to this policy

If the app's behavior changes in a way that affects this policy, the updated version will be published at this URL with a new "Last updated" date.

Contact
